How to Use:

1. Click Start Audio to initialize the audio engine.

2. Click on squares in the sequencer grid to add or remove notes in the pattern.

3. Use the XY pad to control effects:

  • X-axis: Controls vibrato depth and frequency
  • Y-axis: Controls volume LFO frequency and depth

4. Click Play to start the sequence, Stop to pause it.

5. The bottom row controls the bass drum pattern.
